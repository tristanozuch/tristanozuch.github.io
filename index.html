Tristan Ozuch(-Meersseman)

Since September 2020, I am a C.L.E. Moore Instructor at MIT.
Before that I was PhD student in Mathematics at École Normale Supérieure, where I was advised by Olivier Biquard. I graduated from École Normale Supérieure with a Master's degree in Mathematics.


My research is focused on Geometric Analysis and particularly on Einstein manifolds and Ricci flows.
