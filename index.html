Tristan Ozuch(-Meersseman)

I am PhD student in Mathematics at École Normale Supérieure, where I am advised by Olivier Biquard. From September 2020, I will be a C.L.E. Moore Instructor at MIT.
My research is focused on geometric analysis and more precisely on Einstein manifolds and Ricci Flows.
