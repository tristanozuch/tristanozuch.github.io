Tristan Ozuch (-Meersseman)


I am an Assistant Professor at MIT.
Before that (2020-2023), I was a C.L.E. Moore Instructor at MIT.
Before that (2017-2020) I was PhD student in Mathematics at École Normale Supérieure, where I was advised by Olivier Biquard. I graduated from École Normale Supérieure with a Master's degree in Mathematics in 2017.


My research is focused on Geometric Analysis and particularly on Einstein manifolds and Ricci flows.
